COLLEGE CHATS Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose personal information when you use our messaging application.

COLLEGE CHATS is a messaging app where people can message their contacts and convert selected messages into posts.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Mobile Number

We require your mobile number for authentication purposes. We securely store this information and do not share it with third parties.

1.2 Contacts

With your permission, we may access and store your phone contacts to facilitate chatting with your friends. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

1.3 Public Feed

When you publish a chat to the public feed, it becomes visible to other users. Please be mindful of the information you share, as it will be publicly accessible.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your mobile number.
  • Facilitate communication with your contacts.
  • Display chats on the public feed as per your preferences.

3. Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
